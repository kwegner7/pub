What Can You Do On The Mint Website?

You Can See The Total Amount of Credit Card Debt
On the left, click "Overview". The heading "Credit Cards" shows the total amount of credit card debt.
You Can See Recent Transactions For The Priceline Credit Card
On the left, click "Transactions" then pulldown "Credit Cards" the click "PRICELINE".
You Can See Recent Transactions For All Accounts
On the left, click "Transactions" then click "All accounts"
You Can See Recent Transactions For Joint Beyond Banking
On the left, click "Transactions" then click "Investments" then click "Joint Beyond Banking".
You can select date range - pulldown Filters then select Start Date and End Date.

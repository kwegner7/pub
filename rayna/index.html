HOW TO APPLY TO SCHOOLS IN THE US

Each university or college has different admission requirements. These may vary depending upon the department within the university or college.

For example, at the University of Colorado at Colorado Springs (UCCS), one of the graduate department that you might choose is called "Communication". This department is close to your undergraduate experience because it includes specialties like graphic arts, digital media and digital filmmaking.

ADMISSION TO THE UCCS GRADUATE SCHOOL

UNDERGRADUATE REQUIREMENT

You must have an undergraduate grade point average of 2.75 or better ('A' is equal to 4.0).

A transcript of all undergraduate classes and grades must be submitted during the application process.
ENGLISH REQUIREMENT

You must have a score of at least 80 on the TOEFL
OR a score of at least 6.5 on the IELTS test.

Both of these tests can be taken on-line and you can repeat until successful.
